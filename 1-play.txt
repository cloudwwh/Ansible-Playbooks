Ansible Play is a set of instruction expressed in YAML. It targets a host or group of hosts annd provide a series of tasks, which are executed in sequence
Ansible Playbook - Plays are kept in a file called Playbooks, which may contain one or more plays. Each play may have a number of options configured such as remote_user, 
become root and gather_facts
